How we hold your information

Privacy policy.

Plain language, no surprises. If something is unclear, write to us and we will explain.

Robora Therapy Solutions takes your privacy seriously — both as a clinical and a legal duty. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we keep it safe. Last updated: April 2026.

Who we are

Robora Therapy Solutions is a counselling and emotional wellness practice founded by Joanne Aluko, based in Lagos, Nigeria. For the purposes of this policy, “we”, “our”, and “Robora” refer to Robora Therapy Solutions.

What we collect

We collect only what we need to do our work well. That falls into three categories:

  • Inquiry information. When you fill out a booking or contact form, we collect your name, email, phone (if provided), and the content of your message. This goes directly to Joanne and is read by no one else.
  • Clinical records. Once you become a client, we keep session notes, intake assessments, and treatment plans. These are protected by professional ethics and stored securely. They are never shared without your written consent, except as required by law (see “Limits” below).
  • Website analytics. Like most websites, we collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site — pages viewed, approximate location, device type. We do not track you across other websites.

How we use it

We use what we collect to: respond to your inquiry, deliver counselling services, keep clinical records as required by professional standards, and improve our website. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Ever.

How we store it

Inquiry emails are kept in a secure, password-protected inbox. Clinical records are stored in a locked physical file or an encrypted digital system, accessible only to Joanne. Website analytics are anonymised and aggregated.

How long we keep it

Inquiry messages from people who do not become clients are kept for 12 months, then deleted. Clinical records are kept for 7 years from the last session, in line with professional standards for record retention. After that, they are securely destroyed.

Your rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (2023) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Know what we hold about you.
  • Receive a copy of your records.
  • Ask us to correct anything inaccurate.
  • Ask us to delete information that we are not legally required to keep.
  • Withdraw consent for any processing that depends on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, write to hello@roboratherapy.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Limits

There are situations where we may be required by law to disclose information without your consent. These are detailed in our confidentiality notice, and in summary they include: serious risk of harm to you or others, suspected abuse of a child or vulnerable adult, and orders from a court of competent jurisdiction.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies — small text files that make the site work. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party social media trackers. You can disable cookies in your browser without affecting the site's core functionality.

Children's privacy

If you are under 16, please do not submit personal information through this site without a parent or guardian's involvement. When we provide therapy to minors, we maintain separate records and follow specific protocols for parental consent and disclosure, detailed in our confidentiality notice.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here and update the date at the top. Substantive changes will be communicated to current clients directly.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, or if you would like to make a privacy-related request, please write to hello@roboratherapy.com. We will reply personally.
