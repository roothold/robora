Received with care

Thank you for reaching out.

Joanne will read your note herself and reply within one working day. We promise.

In the meantime, take a breath. The hardest part — naming that something needs attention — you have already done.

What happens next

Within one working day, you will receive a personal email from Joanne. It will not be a form letter. It will name what you wrote, ask any clarifying questions, and propose two or three times for a free 20-minute call.

The call itself is unhurried. Twenty minutes to listen, understand, and decide together whether Robora is the right home for what you are carrying. There is no obligation to continue, and no awkwardness if you decide it is not the right fit.

While you wait

If something else comes to mind — a question, a clarification, a story you forgot to mention — you can write to Joanne directly at hello@roboratherapy.com. Or browse our journal for reflections from the practice.

A small reminder

Reaching out is the bravest part. You have done it. The rest is just conversation.
